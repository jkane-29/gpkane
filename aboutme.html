My Journey

My creative journey began with a fascination for both cinema and comedy. I discovered early on that I loved being behind the camera just as much as I enjoyed performing in front of an audience. This dual passion has shaped my unique perspective as both a filmmaker and entertainer.

Throughout my career, I've worked on various video production projects while simultaneously developing my voice as a comedian. Each discipline informs the other - my comedic sensibility enhances my filmmaking, while my technical skills in video production help me create better comedy content.

I'm constantly learning, growing, and pushing myself to explore new creative territories. Whether I'm editing a video at 2 AM or writing new material for an upcoming show, I'm driven by the joy of creating something that resonates with people.
